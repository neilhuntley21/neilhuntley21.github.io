    THOR Schedule

  • Event Duration: This event will take place on a single day.
  • Date: Saturday, October 14, 2023
  • Location: Chapel Hill High School, Chapel Hill, NC
  • Schedule (subject to change):
    • 7:00 am: Teams will load-in and check-in at this time.
    • 9:30 am: There will be a drivers meeting on the field.
    • 10:00 am: The opening/welcome ceremony will take place.
    • 10:15 am to 1:00 pm: Qualification matches will be held.
    • 1:00 pm to 1:30 pm: There will be a lunch break.
    • 1:30 pm to 4 pm: Qualification matches will continue.
    • 4 pm: Alliance selections will take place.
    • 4:30 pm: Play-off and finals matches will be held.
    • 6:00 pm: Awards will be presented.
    • 6:15 pm: Load-out and clean up will take place.
