THOR Schedule

  • Event Duration: This event will take place on a single day.
  • Date: Saturday, October 14, 2023
  • Location: Chapel Hill High School, Chapel Hill, NC
  • Schedule (subject to change):
    • 7:00 am: Teams will load-in and check-in at this time.
    • 9:30 am: There will be a drivers meeting on the field.
    • 10:00 am: The opening/welcome ceremony will take place.
    • 10:15 am to 1:00 pm: Qualification matches will be held.
    • 1:00 pm to 1:30 pm: There will be a lunch break.
    • 1:30 pm to 4 pm: Qualification matches will continue.
    • 4 pm: Alliance selections will take place.
    • 4:30 pm: Play-off and finals matches will be held.
    • 6:00 pm: Awards will be presented.
    • 6:15 pm: Load-out and clean up will take place.

To reiterate, the event is scheduled for Saturday, October 14, 2023 and will be held at Chapel Hill High School in Chapel Hill, NC. The day's activities will begin with team load-in and check-in at 7:00 am. At 9:30 am there will be a drivers meeting on the field. The opening/welcome ceremony is scheduled for 10:00 am. Qualification matches are set to take place from 10:15 am to 1:00 pm and then again from 1:30 pm to 4 pm after a lunch break. Alliance selections are scheduled for 4 pm followed by play-off and finals matches at 4:30 pm. Awards are set to be presented at 6:00 pm and load-out and clean up is scheduled for 6:15 pm.
